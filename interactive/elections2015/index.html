2015 USG Elections Guide

By Will Welch / April 28, 2015

Update: The winners of this year's Undergraduate Student Government elections have been announced. View the candidates who were elected below, marked red.

This year's elections for the Undergraduate Student Government run from Monday, April 27 to Friday, May 1 on SOLAR. The candidates who are elected will control more than $3 million from the $99.50 Student Activity Fee paid by undergraduate students every semester. They will also be responsible for administrating clubs, organizing campus-wide events and representing students on university committees.

Two parties and a contingent of independent candidates seek election this year. The PARTY Party represents most of the incumbent candidates. It is challenged by the HOUSE Party and independent candidates. Changes to the USG constitution, a $5.50 increase of the Student Activity Fee and the option to place a vote of no confidence are also on the ballot this year.
